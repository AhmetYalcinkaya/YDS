# Privacy Policy - YDS 1000 Kelime

Last updated: December 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how YDS 1000 Kelime ("App") handles your personal and usage data.

## Data We Collect
- Usage data (words studied, progress, session timestamps)
- Device information (device model, OS version, app version)
- Crash reports and analytics (for improving the App)

We do NOT collect or store personally identifiable information such as name, email, address, or payment details.

## How We Use Data
- Improve app features and performance
- Provide spaced repetition scheduling and progress tracking
- Diagnose and fix issues

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may share anonymized analytics with service providers to improve the app.

## Storage and Security
Data is stored securely and transmitted over encrypted channels. Keep your device secure and up-to-date.

## Your Rights
You can request export or deletion of your data by contacting us at: privacy@ydsapp.com

## Children
The App is appropriate for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in this document and reflected in the app.
